Admission Process

Our straightforward process makes it easy to join the Lakewood family

Admission Requirements

What you need to prepare for the application process

Application Form

Birth Certificate

Copy of the student's birth certificate for age verification.

Passport Photos

Two recent passport-sized photographs of the student.

Medical Records

Copy of immunization records and any relevant medical information.
