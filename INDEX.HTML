About Our School

Lakewood Schools (Komarock & Kitengela) delivers quality, Christian-based education (8-4-4 curriculum), guided by the motto "Honouring God Through Excellence." It fosters each child's best in a tranquil and safe environment.

Founded in 2010, we have consistently provided excellent education that balances academic excellence with strong Christian values. Our dedicated teachers and staff work tirelessly to ensure every student reaches their full potential.
